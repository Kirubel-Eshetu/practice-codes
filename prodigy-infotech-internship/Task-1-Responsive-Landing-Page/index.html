A story of Jesus Christ

Birthplace: Jesus was born in Bethlehem, Judea, during the reign of Herod the Great.  
Time Period: He lived in the 1st century CE, a time of Roman occupation in the region.
Religious Background: He was Jewish, and his teachings were rooted in Jewish tradition.  

Jesus Christ Ministry

Ministry: He began his public ministry around the age of 30, traveling throughout Galilee and Judea.
Disciples: He gathered a group of 12 disciples who followed him and learned from his teachings.  
Teachings: His teachings emphasized love, forgiveness, humility, and the Kingdom of God.  

Jesus Christ Miracles & Death


Miracles: The Gospels record accounts of Jesus performing miracles, such as healing the sick and feeding the multitudes.  
Crucifixion: He was crucified in Jerusalem under the Roman governor Pontius Pilate.
Resurrection: Jesus Christ rose from the dead three days after his crucifixion. Jesus died for the sin of the world.
